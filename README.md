<h1 align="center">🏥 Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard (Excel)</h1>

<h2>📊 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
The <b>Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard</b> is an Excel-based data analytics project designed
to analyze emergency department operations and visualize key healthcare metrics.
</p>

<p>
Hospitals generate large volumes of operational data every day. Without structured analysis,
administrators struggle to monitor patient flow, waiting times, admission rates,
and department workload.
</p>

<p>
This project transforms raw emergency room data into an <b>interactive Excel dashboard</b>
that provides insights to support <b>data-driven decision making</b>.
</p>

<h2>🎯 Business Problem</h2>

<ul>
<li>High patient inflow in emergency departments</li>
<li>Long patient waiting times</li>
<li>Uneven department workload</li>
<li>Lack of clear insights from raw hospital data</li>
</ul>

<p>
This dashboard converts raw hospital data into clear visual insights
to help administrators improve operational efficiency.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)</h2>

<ul>
<li>Total Number of Patients</li>
<li>Average Waiting Time</li>
<li>Admission vs Non-Admission Cases</li>
<li>Patient Satisfaction Score</li>
<li>Patient Age Group Distribution</li>
<li>Department Referrals</li>
<li>Monthly Patient Trends</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Dashboard Analysis</h2>

<h3>1️⃣ Patient Demographics</h3>
<p>
Analyzes patient distribution by age group and demographics
to understand which population segments frequently visit
the emergency department.
</p>

<h3>2️⃣ Waiting Time Analysis</h3>
<p>
Shows average patient waiting time and how it changes
during peak patient inflow periods.
</p>

<h3>3️⃣ Department Referral Analysis</h3>
<p>
Displays which hospital departments receive the highest
referrals from emergency cases.
</p>
